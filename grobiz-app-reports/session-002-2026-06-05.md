### ⚠️ OpenPanel caveat (flagged, not silently patched)
grobiz's `lib/analytics.ts` constructs `new OpenPanel({ clientId })` with **no `apiUrl`** → the `@openpanel/web` SDK defaults to OpenPanel **cloud**, so events won't reach self-hosted `analytics.biji.uk` until grobiz sets `apiUrl: 'https://analytics.biji.uk'`. The client IDs are wired and the projects exist; this is a one-line app change for the team to own (docs/17 says "client-side surfaces need only the Client ID" — that's the gap).
