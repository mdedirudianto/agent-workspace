## Gotcha — `db:push` in deploy.sh is non-interactive-hostile
`deployment/pm2/scripts/deploy.sh` runs `drizzle-kit push`, which **re-proposes the `feature_flags` NULLS-NOT-DISTINCT unique index every run** and shows an interactive truncate prompt — under a non-TTY SSH/CI run this aborts the deploy (it aborted *before* the restart this session, leaving the build staged but the old process running until I restarted manually). Recommend the team switch deploy.sh to `drizzle-kit migrate` (apply committed migrations) or make the index diff stable. Until then: skip `db:push` on redeploys with no `apps/api/src/db/` change.
