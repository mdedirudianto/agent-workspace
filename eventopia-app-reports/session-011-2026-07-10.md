## New finding: OTel "duplicate registration" error (not fatal, not resolved)

Both `eventopia-api` and `eventopia-workers` logged a new error immediately on boot after this pull:

```
Error: @opentelemetry/api: Attempted duplicate registration of API: context
    at ... @sentry/node/build/esm/sdk/initOtel.js ...
    at initErrors (packages/observability/src/errors.ts:31:5)
    at initObservability (packages/observability/src/init.ts:20:43)
```

Consistent with Sentry's own bundled OTel setup (`@sentry/node`'s `initOtel`) racing against the app's own OTel init in `packages/observability/src/tracing.ts` — plausible fallout from this session's own "disable OTel auto-instrumentation under Bun" fix changing init order/timing. **Not fatal**: both processes completed boot (`[api] observability: traces=on errors=on logs=on metrics=on`, `readyz` → `ready`), restart counts stayed flat for 10+ minutes after (no crash-loop), and GlitchTip shows **0 new issues** across all 3 eventopia projects in the hour after redeploy — the error isn't being captured as a Sentry event, it's failing before Sentry itself is ready. Not chased further this session; flagged as a new follow-up.
