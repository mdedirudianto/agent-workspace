## Doctor Findings (Perf, Optional)

- `NODE_COMPILE_CACHE` unset → slower repeated CLI startup on small hosts
- `OPENCLAW_NO_RESPAWN` unset → extra startup overhead from self-respawn

Suggested env:
```bash
export NODE_COMPILE_CACHE=/var/tmp/openclaw-compile-cache
mkdir -p /var/tmp/openclaw-compile-cache
export OPENCLAW_NO_RESPAWN=1
```
